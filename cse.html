The Computer Science department in an engineering college typically offers a range of undergraduate and graduate programs that focus on computer programming, software development, computer systems and architecture, artificial intelligence, machine learning, data science, cybersecurity, and other related fields.

The department's curriculum is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in computer science theory and practice, as well as hands-on experience working on real-world projects. Faculty members are often accomplished researchers and industry experts who are actively engaged in cutting-edge research, development, and innovation.
